EzaXpress

Freelance Web Developer · Devllope

The Challenge

EzaXpress needed a professional online presence that reflected the reliability of a transport and logistics operator, with no existing website and limited technical resources in-house.

The Solution

I designed and built a clean, mobile-first website from scratch — clear service pages, an easy enquiry path and consistent branding — so customers can quickly understand what EzaXpress offers and how to reach them.

My Role

  • End-to-end design and development
  • Mobile-responsive layout and cross-browser testing
  • Client communication and revisions

Tech Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, responsive design principles.
